The sky speaks in a language older than memory. Every planet in its orbit, every lunar phase completing its arc — these are not coincidences. They are invitations. And if you have found your way here, the cosmos have something to say to you.

Lady Luna began as a whisper during a new moon in Scorpio. What started as a personal practice of chart readings and late-night journaling has grown into a community of women learning to use the stars as a mirror — not a map, but a mirror. The distinction matters. The stars do not control your life. They reflect it back to you with unexpected clarity.

Here you will find weekly lunar guidance, deep-dive astrological essays, and a space to explore your natal chart as a living, breathing document of who you are becoming. The cosmos are always in motion. So are you.
